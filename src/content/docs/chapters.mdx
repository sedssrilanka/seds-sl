# Managing Chapters

SEDS Chapters represent the individual university or institutional branches of SEDS Sri Lanka. This guide shows how to add, edit, and manage these branches.

![Chapters Admin Panel](https://placehold.co/800x400/0f172a/ffffff?text=Payload+CMS:+Chapters)

## 1. Navigating to the Chapters Collection

1. Go to the Admin dashboard by clicking the **Admin** link in the navigation bar (if you are logged in) or by navigating to `/admin`.
2. On the left sidebar sidebar, under the **Collections** section, click on **Chapters**.
3. You will see a list of all existing chapters.

## 2. Creating a New Chapter

1. Click the **"Create New"** button in the top right corner.
2. **Basic Information:**
   - **Name**: The full name of the chapter (e.g., "SEDS UOC").
   - **Slug**: Generates automatically. This is used in the URL (e.g., `/chapters/seds-uoc`).
   - **Main Image**: Upload a high-quality featured image representing the university or chapter.
   - **Description**: A short summary that appears on cards and search results.
3. **Contact & Social:**
   - Input the `Contact Email` for the chapter.
   - Add as many social media links (Facebook, LinkedIn, etc.) as needed by clicking "Add Social Link".
4. **Rich Content:**
   - The **Content** section uses a block-based editor. You can mix text, images, and other media to describe the chapter's history in detail.
5. **Gallery (Optional):**
   - Add images to the image gallery block to showcase events or members.

## 3. Saving and Publishing

Once you have filled out the information, look to the right-side panel:

- Click **"Save Draft"** if you are not ready for it to be public.
- Click **"Publish"** when you want it visible on the live website.
